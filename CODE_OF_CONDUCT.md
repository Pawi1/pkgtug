# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project is a professional, technical community. We expect participants to behave accordingly.

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Critique ideas, not people
- Accept and give feedback in good faith
- Stay on topic — this is a technical project

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment
- Publishing someone's private information without permission
- Sustained disruption of discussions
- Any other conduct that a reasonable person would consider unprofessional

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate these standards, and may ban participants who repeatedly or seriously breach them.

Incidents can be reported to: pawel.rutkowski@vcn.pl. Reports will be handled promptly and confidentially.
